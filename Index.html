WHAT WE DO AT REBIRTH


Rebirth allows you to practice yoga on a floating platform while enjoying the beauty of nature- the sky above, the ocean below, and peace within. The purpose of yoga is to build strength, awareness and harmony in both the mind and body. Yoga also provides a retreat from the chaotic and busy lives. Yoga develops inner awareness. Yoga reduces the tension and stress in the mind and body and we at rebirth try to bring all these benefits of yoga to you in a more calm and comfortable manner.

NO STRESS

Yoga is known for its ability to ease stress and promote relaxation.In fact, multiple studies have shown that it can decrease the secretion of cortisol, the primary stress hormone.

MORE STRENGTH

Yoga is a great addition to an exercise routine for its strength building benefits. In fact, there are specific poses in yoga that are designed to increase strength and build muscle.

MORE FLEXIBILITY

Many people add yoga to their fitness routine to improve flexibility and balance. Yoga can optimize performance through the use of specific poses that target flexibility and balance.

NO STRESS

From pumping blood throughout the body to supplying tissues with important nutrients, Studies show that yoga may help improve heart health and reduce several risk factors for heart disease.
